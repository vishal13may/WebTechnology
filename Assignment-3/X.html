Design

Pig is made up of two components: the first is the language itself,which is called PigLatin (yes, people naming various Hadoop projects to tend to have a sense of humor associated with their naming conventions), and the second is a runtime environment where PigLatin programs are executed.Think of the relaiton between a Java Virtual Machine(JVM) and a Java application. In this section,we'll just refer to the whole entity as Pig.
